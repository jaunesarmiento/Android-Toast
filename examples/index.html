Android Toast Example

Click here for example 1 (defaults):
var toast1 = new Android_Toast();
toast1.set_content('Please check your connection and try again.');
toast1.show();

Click here for example 2 (customizing position & duration):
var toast2 = new Android_Toast();
toast2.set_content('Connected to Wi-Fi network Router Name');
toast2.set_duration(4000);
toast2.set_position('top');
toast2.show();

Note that these examples are best viewed at a width smaller than 600px (to simulate a mobile device)
